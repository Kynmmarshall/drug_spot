Features

Everything You Need to Find Medicine

Powerful tools for patients and pharmacies, designed for the Cameroon healthcare landscape.

🔍

Smart Medicine Search

Search by name, filter by distance and price. Find exactly what you need at the closest pharmacy.

🗺️

Community Map

See all pharmacies on an interactive map with real-time distance calculations from your location.

💊

Pharmacy Management

Pharmacy owners can add, edit, and manage their medicine inventory directly from the app.

💰

Price Transparency

Compare medicine prices across pharmacies. No hidden costs — see FCFA pricing upfront.

🌙

Dark & Light Themes

Easy on the eyes, day or night. Toggle between beautifully designed light and dark modes.

🌍

Bilingual Support

Fully translated in English and French — switch languages instantly from anywhere in the app.

How It Works

Get Your Medicine in 4 Simple Steps

Whether you're a patient or pharmacy owner, getting started takes under a minute.

1

Create Account

Sign up as a patient or pharmacy owner with your email and phone number.

2

Enable Location

Allow location access so we can show pharmacies and distances near you.

3

Search Medicines

Browse or search for the medicine you need. Filter by price and distance.

4

Visit Pharmacy

Get directions to the nearest pharmacy that has your medicine in stock.
